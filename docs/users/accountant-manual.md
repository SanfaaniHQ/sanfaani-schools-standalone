# Accountant Manual

Accountant support provides the Stage 10 online school-fees foundation.

## Current Responsibilities

- Create fee items for the school.
- Assign fees to classes, sessions, terms, or selected students.
- Generate student or class invoices.
- Record positive manual payments against outstanding invoices.
- Review invoice balances and student finance history.
- Use guided onboarding steps for finance setup.

Finance records are restricted to the active school. Teachers, Result Officers, Students, and Parents do not receive finance management access.

## Planned

Advanced finance reports and audit views are planned for Stage 11. Import/export is planned for Stage 12. Payment gateway automation, offline fee capture, parent/student finance portals, double-entry ledgers, payroll, and procurement are not implemented.

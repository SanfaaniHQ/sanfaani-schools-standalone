# Product Overview

## Purpose

Sanfaani Schools is a school management and result checking platform for schools that need simple, controlled, online result operations. It helps schools manage students, upload or enter results, publish results securely, and let parents check published results online.

## Target Schools

- Conventional nursery, primary, and secondary schools.
- Islamic schools and madrasahs.
- Training centres.
- Small and growing private schools.
- Schools that need flexible grading and affordable result access.

## Core Modules

- Authentication and roles.
- Super Admin dashboard.
- School management.
- School Admin dashboard.
- Classes, subjects, sessions, and terms.
- Students and Student 360 profile.
- Manual result entry.
- CSV/Excel result upload.
- Grading scales.
- Result publishing and unpublishing.
- Scratch card request, approval, generation, usage, and revoke flow.
- Public result checker.
- Result verification foundation.
- Lead capture from public contact and demo pages.
- Multilingual foundation for English, French, and Arabic.

## User Roles

- Super Admin: manages platform-wide schools, plans, feature access, payments, and scratch card approvals.
- School Admin: manages school setup, students, staff, results, publishing, and scratch card requests.
- Result Officer: supports result entry/upload/review where enabled.
- Teacher: future role for class/subject result work.
- Parent: future account role; today parents use public result checker.
- Student: future portal role; today student identity is the admission number.

## Business Model

The production launch can support flexible pricing by student, term, session, or custom school agreement. Manual payment and scratch card access are active. Paystack, Flutterwave, parent direct payment, and school-paid result access are prepared as future upgrade paths.

## Roadmap

- PDF result generation.
- QR image generation.
- CBT and assessment/test results.
- Parent direct payment.
- School-paid result access.
- SMS notifications.
- Mobile app.
- Biometric attendance.
- VPS migration when scale or background jobs require more control.

# School Reports Operations

The Reports Center gives School Admins one read-only place to scan cross-module school activity and jump into existing detailed reports.

## Access

- School Admin: full Reports Center access.
- Super Admin: access only when operating inside a school context.
- Teacher: no full Reports Center access by default.
- Accountant: existing finance reports remain available; no full Reports Center access by default.
- Result Officer: existing result and CBT workflows remain available; no full Reports Center access by default.
- Student and Parent: no Reports Center access.

## Workflow

1. Open **Reports Center** from the school dashboard or sidebar.
2. Apply date, class, session, term, or status filters as needed.
3. Review the overview cards for the school.
4. Open the linked detailed module report for deeper work.
5. Use existing CSV export links only when the current role and module allow them.

## Modules Covered

- Students and academic setup.
- Admissions.
- Attendance and offline sync.
- Finance.
- LMS and CBT.
- Live classes.
- Communications.
- High-level operations health.

## Safe Data Handling

The center shows counts, amounts, statuses, and high-level readiness labels. It does not show raw sensitive records such as CBT answers, admission documents, payment references, meeting passwords, provider credentials, notification payloads, backup file paths, or update internals.

## Deferred Boundaries

The Reports Center is not a BI engine, custom report builder, public reporting portal, PDF redesign, report scheduler, delivery system, or cross-school analytics layer.

# White-Label Release Checklist

- Confirm white-label license mode and entitlement.
- Confirm branding settings are configured safely.
- Confirm logo/favicon uploads are safe public assets.
- Confirm no SVG or executable upload is accepted.
- Confirm email footer, login, dashboard, and report branding are reviewed.
- Confirm marketplace buyer checklist reflects white-label scope.
- Confirm private paths and secrets are not shown in UI or emails.

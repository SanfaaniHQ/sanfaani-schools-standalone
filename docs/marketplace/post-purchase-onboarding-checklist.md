# Post-Purchase Onboarding Checklist

Use this checklist after marketplace purchase or direct sale.

## Buyer Intake

- Confirm buyer name, school name, domain, hosting provider, and contact email.
- Confirm license mode and support plan.
- Confirm whether installation is self-service, assisted, managed, or white-label.

## Setup Support

- Share buyer installation checklist.
- Confirm `.env.marketplace.example` was copied to `.env`.
- Confirm database and SMTP placeholders were replaced by buyer-owned values.
- Confirm installer completion.
- Confirm license activation or validation.

## Operational Handover

- Confirm backup responsibility.
- Confirm update responsibility.
- Confirm support channel.
- Confirm staff/admin training path.
- Confirm onboarding checklist progress.

## First-Week Review

- Verify login and school profile.
- Verify students, classes, subjects, sessions, and terms.
- Verify result workflow.
- Verify public result checker.
- Verify backup metadata and manual database export.

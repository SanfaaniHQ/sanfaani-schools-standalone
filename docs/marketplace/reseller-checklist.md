# Reseller Checklist

This checklist supports partners and resellers without giving them unsafe access or unclear promises.

## Sales Readiness

- Confirm reseller license terms and territory.
- Share marketplace listing copy and feature boundaries.
- Provide approved screenshots and demo script.
- Provide hosting requirements and buyer installation checklist.
- Confirm support escalation path.

## Delivery Readiness

- Use a clean package validated by `marketplace:validate-package`.
- Do not include `.env`, logs, backups, private uploads, test databases, or generated archives.
- Provide buyer-safe `.env.marketplace.example`.
- Provide license activation guidance.
- Provide update and backup guidance.

## Handover

- Record buyer domain, license mode, support contact, and installation date.
- Confirm who manages hosting, SMTP, backups, updates, and renewals.
- Do not promise automated restore, marketplace sync, deployment automation, or billing automation unless separately contracted.

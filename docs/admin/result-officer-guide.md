# Result Officer Guide

## Login

Result Officers log in with email or `staff_code` and password. Do not use student admission numbers for staff login.

## Student Access

Result Officers can view student lists and Student 360 profiles where permissions allow.

## Result Entry

Use manual result entry for direct score entry. Keep subject, class, session, term, score, grade, and remarks accurate before review.

## Result Upload

Use CSV/Excel upload for class-based result work. Follow the current template. Admission numbers identify students in result files.

## Review Status

Result Officers should help prepare and review results, but publishing/unpublishing may remain limited to School Admin users depending on the school policy.

## Limitations

Result Officers should not manage scratch card approval, platform plans, school billing, or Super Admin settings.

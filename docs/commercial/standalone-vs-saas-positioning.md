# Standalone Vs SaaS Positioning

Use this guide when deciding whether a buyer should hear the standalone offer, the hosted SaaS conversation, or a managed private installation offer.

## Quick Positioning

| Path | Best for | Buyer gets | Important boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Standalone | One private school that wants its own installation | Dedicated Laravel portal, installer/license readiness, school branding, support docs, local operational control | Not SaaS billing or a public marketplace automation flow |
| SaaS | Schools that want Sanfaani-hosted access | Hosted service conversation, central operations, subscription/commercial path | Full automated billing must not be claimed unless implemented and approved |
| Managed private installation | School wants private installation but needs Sanfaani/partner operations help | Done-for-you setup, guided handoff, support owner, deployment assistance | Managed automation depends on contract scope |

## Standalone Sales Language

Use:

- "Dedicated installation for one private school."
- "Laravel remains the source of truth."
- "Your school controls its portal, users, records, branding, and local support process."
- "Guided update review and backup readiness are included as support foundations."
- "Done-for-you installation can be scoped for non-technical schools."

Avoid:

- "Full SaaS billing is ready."
- "One-click marketplace installation."
- "Auto-updates from an online update server."
- "Remote license server enforcement."
- "Full offline portal."
- "Google Meet, Zoom, or Teams rooms are created automatically."

## Buyer Path Questions

- Does the school want its own installation or hosted access?
- Who will own hosting, backups, domain, SSL, mail, and support?
- Does the buyer need source/package ownership or managed service access?
- Are they comfortable with technical setup, or do they need done-for-you implementation?
- Are they buying school operations, learning digitization, white-label polish, or a managed private deployment?

## Offer Separation

Standalone offer:

- Product license or commercial access.
- Installation and activation path.
- Support/handoff package.
- Optional managed setup and training.

SaaS offer:

- Hosted access path.
- Platform-managed operation.
- Subscription/billing terms when business-approved.
- No source/package handoff by default.

Managed private installation:

- Private standalone deployment.
- Sanfaani or partner support.
- Deployment, backup, update, and handoff responsibilities defined in contract.

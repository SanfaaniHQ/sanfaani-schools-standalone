# Pricing And Plan Positioning

This guide describes packaging tiers without hardcoding final prices. Exact pricing, legal terms, support SLA, renewal rules, country-specific tax treatment, and payment handling are business decisions outside this code stage.

## Pricing Principles

- Price the value of school operations, implementation effort, support scope, and maintenance responsibility.
- Keep standalone positioned as a private school installation, not the same offer as hosted SaaS.
- Separate license, hosting, implementation, training, support, customization, and renewal terms.
- State limitations before the buyer commits.

## Starter School

Best for schools that need the core school portal first.

Suggested inclusion:

- Core school setup.
- Students, classes, subjects, sessions, and terms.
- Admissions foundation.
- Attendance.
- Basic reports and school dashboards.
- Branding basics.
- Backup/update readiness docs.

Positioning note: Starter should not be sold as a finance, LMS, live-class, or enterprise support package unless those modules are included by the business owner.

## Growth School

Best for schools that want operations and fee control.

Suggested inclusion:

- Everything in Starter School.
- Finance/fees.
- Import/export.
- Communication Center.
- Reports Pack.
- Support runbooks.
- Installer/license readiness.

Positioning note: Growth includes manual finance operations and reports. It is not a full ERP or payment gateway automation package.

## Digital Learning School

Best for schools adding learning content and assessment support.

Suggested inclusion:

- Everything in Growth School.
- LMS.
- CBT integration.
- Manual live classes.
- Provider abstraction foundation.
- Learning support docs.

Positioning note: Digital Learning uses manual meeting links. It does not include Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams API automation.

## Enterprise Private Installation

Best for schools that need a polished private deployment and stronger handoff.

Suggested inclusion:

- Everything in Digital Learning School.
- White-label polish where licensed.
- Guided updates.
- Backup/restore hardening.
- Release handoff.
- Priority support.
- Custom deployment assistance.

Positioning note: Enterprise can include stronger service commitments, but automation boundaries still apply unless separately built, tested, and contracted.

## Common Paid Add-Ons

- Done-for-you installation.
- Data migration or cleanup.
- Staff training.
- Custom report work.
- Custom module configuration.
- Hosting/server management.
- Domain, SSL, and mail setup.
- Custom feature development after written scope approval.

## Not Automatically Included

- Unlimited custom feature development.
- School-fee payment gateway integration.
- External provider API credentials or provider contracts.
- Manual data entry or migration unless scoped.
- Hosting, domain, SSL, SMS, WhatsApp, email provider, or payment processor costs.
- Legal compliance guarantees.
- Enforcement of the school's internal policies.
